About Us

The Rutgers Creative Writing Club is geared towards writers of all majors and skill levels.
We wish to provide a space for students with a strong interest in writing to freely discuss ideas, receive feedback on their work, participate in activities to improve and expand on their projects, collaborate with fellow writers, and express themselves through creative writing.
